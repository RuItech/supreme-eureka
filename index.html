About

As RU i-Tech we value innovation, team work, honesty, equality, leadership, respect, Integrity of service and Technology. We pride ourselves to working as a team to achieve success.

History

RU i-Tech was started in 16 th June 2015 by Godfrey Njoroge and Bhagyashree Halai. At first, the purpose of the organization was to bring together students from the School of Computing Sciences in a bid increase working together plus learning from and interacting with each other. Later, however, the organization evolved and became inclusive of all Riara University students. Thus, our purpose has evolved into nurturing and honing the skills we gain from our lecturer rooms to create solutions for the problems faced in our communities.

The organization is planning to extend its boundaries outside Riara University
